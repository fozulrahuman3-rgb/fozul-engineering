Concrete Investigation Services

Non-destructive testing, structural assessment, and concrete durability evaluation for quality verification and failure investigation.

Testing & Assessment Methods

Non-Destructive Testing (NDT)

FAHEEM utilizes field-proven NDT methods to assess concrete condition without damaging structures:

Rebound Hammer Testing – Assessment of concrete surface hardness and comparative strength estimation

Ultrasonic Pulse Velocity (UPV) – Measurement of concrete uniformity and detection of voids or cracks

Visual Crack Mapping – Documentation of crack patterns, widths, and distribution for analysis

Rebar Corrosion Evaluation – Assessment of reinforcement condition and corrosion risk

Concrete Condition Assessment

We provide comprehensive evaluation of concrete structures for:

  • Quality verification and specification compliance

Root Cause Analysis Services

Engineering investigation of defects, failures, and performance issues—identifying root causes and developing practical remediation strategies.

Investigation Scope

Types of Failures Investigated

  • Concrete Defects – Cracking, spalling, scaling, efflorescence, and durability issues
  • Structural Failures – Settlement, deflection, or loss of structural integrity
  • Construction Defects – Workmanship issues, material non-compliance, or specification violations
  • Environmental Damage – Corrosion, chemical attack, freeze-thaw damage, or biological deterioration
  • Equipment/System Failures – Performance degradation or operational failures

Investigation Methodology

Phase 1: Visual Assessment & Documentation

Comprehensive site inspection with photographic documentation, condition mapping, and initial hypothesis formation.

Phase 2: Testing & Sampling

Collection of representative samples, non-destructive testing, and material testing to support root cause analysis.

Phase 3: Analysis & Findings

Technical analysis of test results and field observations to identify root cause(s) and contributing factors.

Phase 4: Remediation Recommendations

Practical repair strategies with implementation approach, timeline, and cost implications.

Deliverables

Root cause analysis investigations produce comprehensive technical reports suitable for:

  • Insurance claims and litigation support
  • Regulatory agency submission and compliance documentation
  • Internal management briefing and decision-making
  • Warranty claim resolution and contractor liability assessment
