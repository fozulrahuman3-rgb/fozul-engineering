## 9. ROBOTS.TXT STRATEGY

### Crawl Instructions
- **Allow**: All important pages and assets
- **Disallow**: Admin areas, temporary files, sensitive data
- **Crawl-delay**: Balanced to not overload server
- **Request-rate**: Respectful crawl rate (50-100 requests/hour)

### Blocked Bots
- Aggressive scrapers: AhrefsBot, SemrushBot, DotBot
- Allowed: Google, Bing, LinkedIn, Twitter, WhatsApp bots

### Sitemap Reference
- Points to primary and supplementary sitemaps
- Updates when new content published
